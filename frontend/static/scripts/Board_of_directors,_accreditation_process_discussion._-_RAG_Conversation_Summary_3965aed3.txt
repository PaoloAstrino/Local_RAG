The board of directors plays a crucial role in ensuring data protection and compliance. They are responsible for overseeing and potentially updating regulations concerning personal data, aligning with GDPR standards. The board includes department governance, the EQUIS project leader, and members of the EQUIS team. A key function related to external validation is managing the accreditation process, a rigorous undertaking involving the creation of reports, data sheets, and online repositories. This process, involving both internal work and external review, strengthens the visibility and appeal of programs offered by the institution.
